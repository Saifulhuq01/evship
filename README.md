## What is this?

Every SaaS company that sends webhooks has to build reliable delivery 
infrastructure from scratch — retries, signatures, fan-out, dead letters, 
monitoring. It takes 4–6 engineer-weeks and adds zero business value.

Evship is the open-source, self-hostable alternative to Svix.
MIT licensed. Full feature parity between self-hosted and cloud.

## Why?

- Svix charges $490/month minimum
- Convoy is no longer actively maintained  
- Nothing MIT-licensed works reliably in production
